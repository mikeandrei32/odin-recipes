Cordon Bleu Recipes

Description

Cordon Bleu is a crispy, golden breaded chicken fillet stuffed with ham and cheese, offering a delicious mix of savory, creamy, and crunchy flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • 4 slices cooked ham
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 7x11-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.
  3. Spinkle each piece of chiken on both sides with salt and pepper. Place 1 Swiss cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast.
  4. Roll up each and secure with toothpicks
  5. Place in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink, 30 to 35 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven, and place 1/2 Swiss cheese skice on top of each breast.
  8. Return to the oven until cheese has melted, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove toothpicks and serve immediately.
